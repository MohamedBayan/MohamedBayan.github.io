I am a Research Assistant in Natural Language Processing and Multimodal AI at the Qatar Computing Research Institute (QCRI). My work centers on multimodal language understanding, combating digital disinformation, Arabic language technology, and reinforcement learning for post-training large language models.

I recently completed my MSc in Computing at Qatar University (GPA 4.0/4.0) and hold a BSc in Computer Engineering (GPA 3.99/4.0, ranked 1st in class). My research has appeared at ACL-tier venues including NAACL, EMNLP, ACL, and WWW.

Research Interests

I build and study models that understand language and vision jointly, with a focus on socially impactful problems — disinformation, hateful content, and equitable technology for Arabic and other under-served languages.

🖼️

Multimodal NLP

Vision-language models for memes, hateful content detection, and propaganda analysis across languages and modalities.

🛡️

Content Moderation & Disinformation

Explainable detection of hateful and propagandistic content to support safer, more trustworthy online ecosystems.

🌍

Arabic Language Technology

Datasets, benchmarks, and specialized multilingual LLMs that advance Arabic and low-resource NLP.

🎯

RL for LLM Post-Training

Reinforcement learning (GRPO) with chain-of-thought supervision for reasoning and explainable multimodal detection.
